Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2025

1. Who We Are

KanActive AI is a software project focused on helping individuals and businesses protect sensitive data during use of browser-based AI tools.

2. What We Collect

When you visit this site, we may collect limited anonymous data via Google Analytics, including:

  • Page views and traffic patterns
  • Browser and device type
  • Anonymized IP address (truncated before storage)
  • Time spent on page

We do not collect names, emails, or any personally identifying information through this website unless submitted through a web form.

3. Why We Collect It

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our website so we can improve content, usability, and performance.

4. Consent and Cookies

This site displays a small popup asking for your permission before enabling Google Analytics. If you click “Accept,” a cookie-less tracking script will load. If you decline, no tracking occurs.

5. Legal Basis

For visitors in the European Economic Area (EEA), we rely on your consent under the GDPR. Consent is not required to browse this site, and you may withdraw it at any time by clearing your browser’s localStorage settings.

6. Third Parties

We use Google Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC. Google may process your data on servers in the United States. You can learn more about how Google handles data here.

7. Data Retention

We do not store personal data. Aggregate analytics data may be retained by Google according to their policies, typically for 14 months.

8. Your Rights

If you're located in the EU, UK, or California, you have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Since we don’t store personal data ourselves, these rights primarily apply to data held by Google Analytics. You can opt out of Google Analytics at any time.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, please contact us using this web form..
